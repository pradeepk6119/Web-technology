Places to visit in India

Bengaluru
Bengaluru (formerly Bangalore) is the capital of Karnataka, recognized as India's premier technology hub and "Silicon Valley," with a population of over 8 million. Located in South India, it is a fast-growing metropolis known for its pleasant climate, widespread gardens (like Cubbon Park), and a fusion of modern tech parks with historic landmarks.
Kashmir
Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is a stunning region in northern India known for its breathtaking Himalayan landscapes, serene lakes (like Dal and Nigeen), and rich cultural heritage. It features lush valleys, traditional houseboats, and a distinct blend of Persian, Mughal, and Indian influences in its crafts and cuisine.
Rishikesh
Rishikesh, located in Uttarakhand in the Himalayan foothills, is renowned as the "Yoga Capital of the World" and a major spiritual center situated on the banks of the Ganges. It offers a mix of ancient ashrams, meditation centers, and thrilling adventure sports like white-water rafting, serving as the gateway to the Char Dham pilgrimage.
kashi
Kashi, also known as Varanasi or Banaras, is one of the world's oldest continuously inhabited cities. Located on the banks of the sacred Ganges in Uttar Pradesh, India, it is considered the holiest city for Hindus—the "City of Light" and an eternal abode of Lord Shiva.
