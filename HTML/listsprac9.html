Animals

Lion
Known as the "King of the Jungle," the lion is a large cat with a powerful roar. They live in groups called prides and are found primarily in African savannas. Lions are carnivores known for their hunting skills and majestic manes on males.
Elephant
Elephants are the largest land animals on earth, known for their immense size and intelligence. They use their long trunks for breathing, lifting water, and grasping objects. These herbivores have long tusks and live in complex social structures in forests and grasslands.
Dog
Dogs are loyal, domesticated pets that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. They are known for their varied breeds, keen senses, and ability to be trained for protection or assistance. They are highly social mammals that communicate through barking and tail wagging.
Tiger
Tigers are the largest members of the cat family, characterized by their dark vertical stripes on orange-brown fur. They are apex predators found in Asian forests and are skilled swimmers and hunters. Tigers are solitary animals that rely on stealth to stalk their prey.
